UCR Physics Hack Day 2017

Friday, May 12, 2017 and Saturday, May 13th, 2017; 7:00 pm - 5:30 pm; UCR Physics Building

Creating bridges between programming and scientists

UCR Physics Hack Day is the first hack day in UC Riverside that focuses on using data sets in order to create new and innovative projects.

Event Information

UCR Physics Hack Day will be a 26 hour event, where the first 24 hours will be dedicated solely for developing code and having fun. This year, however it is only open to UC Riverside students. We hope to open it to other schools in the future. For more information, contact or .

The event will be in the third floor of the Physics Building (see map below).

How to get here

The event will be held in the Physics building. Parking will be available in Lot 13. Parking is free for drivers with UC parking permits; details to be announced soon. The map below may be helpful for finding the parking lot and the department once you are on campus.
